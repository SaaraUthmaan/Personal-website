About Me

Welcome! I am Saara Uthmaan, a senior at the University of Washington pursuing a dual degree in Informatics and Creative Writing. Currently, I am a researcher in Dr. Leilani Battle’sUW Interactive Data Lab, where I am developing a beginner-friendly D3.js documentation to support AI-Assisted learning and the verification of AI Hallucinations. Alongside Eman Sherif, I also explore how computing students envision culturally responsive help-seeking communities. This quarter, I’m conducting my capstone project on Somali poetry with Dr. Dr. Temi Odumosu.
